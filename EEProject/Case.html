EEE Workshop:

A watchful eye over old machines:

e.g The Electronic and Electrical Engineering (EEE) workshop at the University of Manchester presents an opportunity to embrace and showcase the possibilities of industry 4.0. By engaging with new technology the workshop recieved custom sensors which, when integrated with the Metricx platform, provided the workshop staff with an easy to use, real-time methodology for data monitoring both streamlining their processes and providing an extra safety mechanism between hazardous machines, such as high temperature lazer cutters, and the off-switch!
